Software

Here you can found a list of softwares that I have developed (totally or partially.) It could be published or not.


  • binarizeDF: A simple python function to binarize a pandas dataframe.

  • RIP-MD: a VMD plugin to perform Residue Interaction Network for Molecular Dynamics trajectories. This was programmed under python2 and maybe it will not work in your system. The RIP-MD core for python3 can be found here

  • Human and Mouse netwoks: Reference gene regulatory netoworks for these organism made by the combination of the RegNetwork, TRRUST and DoRothEA repositories

  • Fly T-WEoN: A Cytoscape app that incorporates a filtering method to determine context-specific gene regulatory networks using Drosophila melanogaster epigenomic information

  • bacterial and archaeal regulatory networks: A web interface to visualize and download gene regulatory networks for bacterias and archea
